About Me

Alain Ducasse is one of the most famous chefs of his generation. Originally from the Landes region of France, today he is at the helm of three restaurants in Monaco, Paris and London, each of which has earned three Michelin stars. A chef, a restaurateur, a hotellier and a teacher, Alain Ducasse has spent the past 30 years developing a unique culinary savoir-faire, which he channels into a number of activities in the modern world of food.
